About Noman Group

It was established in 1968. It was the largest vertically integrated textile group of companies in Bangladesh. In 2011, Noman Group opened six textile factories;[5] by that time, the company was operating 19 plants employing about 40,000 people.[6] In 2012, Noman opened a towel factory.[7] By 2015, the company operated 28 factories, and another five new plants were under development.
